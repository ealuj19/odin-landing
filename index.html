Individual Play for Football Dummies
This website is intended for football beginners looking to improve their individual play. Football is a team sport, but if you're getting carried constantly then you may want to improve individually.
Training Drills for Key Attributes
Dribbling is important for acquiring more time before being forced to get rid of the ball.
Shooting is necesarty for finishing scoring opportunities with no passing options available.
Footwork is crucial for attacking and defending as higher levels of football have higher speeds of play.
Goalkeeping in many ways is an individual sport within the sport of football and requires heavy training focus.
